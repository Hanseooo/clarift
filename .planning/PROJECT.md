# Project: Clarift

## 1. Vision & Purpose
**Clarift** is an AI-powered study engine built specifically for Filipino students and review center learners (e.g., nursing, CPA, engineering, medicine). Its core mission is to **turn studying hard into learning effectively**. 

Unlike generic AI chatbots that answer from broad knowledge (and risk hallucinations), Clarift works *exclusively* with a user's own uploaded study material. It forces a structured, active learning loop designed for high-stakes exams.

**The Core Learning Loop:**
1. **Ingest:** User uploads their own PDF, image, or text.
2. **Structure:** Multi-step AI chain generates a structured summary and diagrams.
3. **Evaluate:** Quizzes are generated strictly from the uploaded material.
4. **Diagnose:** The system identifies weak areas based on quiz performance.
5. **Remediate:** Targeted practice drills are generated specifically for those weak topics.

## 2. Target Audience
- **Primary:** Filipino students preparing for board exams.
- **Secondary:** University students with heavy reading loads.

## 3. Business Model
Freemium SaaS model via PayMongo (GCash + Credit Cards).
- **Free:** 3 summaries/day, 3 quizzes/day, 1 targeted practice/day, chat resets every 5 hours, max 5 document uploads.
- **Pro (₱149/month):** 10 summaries/day, 15 quizzes/day, 10 practice sessions/day, unlimited uploads.

## 4. Key Differentiators
- **Strictly Grounded:** Answers and quizzes only come from uploaded files, not general knowledge.
- **Active Recall Engine:** Automatically moves users from reading to testing to remediation.
- **Diagnostic:** Tracks topic-level performance to reveal unknown gaps.
- **Personalized:** Adjusts explanation style (e.g., ELI5, mental models) and formats based on user settings.
